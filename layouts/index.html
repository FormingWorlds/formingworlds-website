The Forming Worlds Lab

Led by Tim Lichtenberg at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute, University of Groningen, we study the coupled interior–atmosphere evolution of rocky planets during and after their formation.

Our work bridges planetary science, geophysics, and astrophysics, using open-source numerical frameworks like PROTEUS to simulate magma ocean solidification, volatile outgassing, and atmospheric escape.
