## Student Projects

We offer research projects for bachelor's and master's students at the University of Groningen and beyond. Projects are available in the following areas:

### Example Project Topics

- **Magma ocean solidification**: Model the thermal evolution of magma oceans on exoplanets
- **Atmospheric outgassing**: Simulate volatile release during planetary formation
- **Exoplanet observability**: Predict observable signatures of magma ocean planets for JWST
- **Interior–atmosphere coupling**: Develop or extend components of the PROTEUS framework
- **Data analysis**: Analyse JWST observations of rocky exoplanet atmospheres

### How to Apply

If you are interested in a project, please [contact Tim Lichtenberg](mailto:tim.lichtenberg@rug.nl) with:

- Your CV
- A brief description of your research interests
- Your preferred project area (if any)
- Your expected start date and duration
