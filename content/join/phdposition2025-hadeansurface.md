## PhD Position: Hadean Surface Evolution of Rocky Planets

This PhD project focuses on understanding the surface conditions of rocky planets during their earliest evolution — the Hadean era. Using coupled interior–atmosphere models, the project will explore how magma ocean solidification, volatile outgassing, and surface weathering interact to shape the transition from molten to solid surfaces.

### Key Questions

- How quickly do magma oceans solidify on different types of rocky planets?
- What surface conditions emerge after solidification?
- How do these early conditions set the stage for habitability?

### Requirements

- MSc in physics, astrophysics, planetary science, or a related field
- Strong computational skills (Python)
- Interest in planetary geophysics and early Earth evolution
