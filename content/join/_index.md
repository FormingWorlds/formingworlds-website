We are building an international team of researchers and students who are passionate about understanding how planets form and evolve. On the following pages you can find information on how to join the group, funding opportunities, and what life in Groningen is like. All our group members are expected to adhere to the [Forming Worlds Lab Code of Conduct](/code-of-conduct/).
