## Postdoctoral Positions

We periodically have openings for postdoctoral researchers. Current and upcoming opportunities will be listed here.

### Fellowship Opportunities

If there are no open positions, we encourage strong candidates to apply for external fellowships to join our group:

- **Veni** (NWO) — For researchers up to 3 years after PhD
- **Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellowships** (EU) — For international researchers
- **Rubicon** (NWO) — For recently graduated Dutch PhD holders
- **Humboldt Research Fellowship** — For international researchers

We are happy to support fellowship applications. Please [contact us](/contact/) to discuss.
