## PhD Positions 2024

*These positions have been filled. The information below is kept for archival purposes.*

Two PhD positions were available in the Forming Worlds Lab at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute, starting in 2024. The projects focused on modelling the coupled interior–atmosphere evolution of rocky exoplanets.

The application deadline was December 2, 2024. Interviews were held between January 27 and February 7, 2025.
