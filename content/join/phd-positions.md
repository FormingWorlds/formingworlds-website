## PhD Positions in the Forming Worlds Lab

We regularly offer PhD positions at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute, University of Groningen. Our PhD projects focus on the formation and early evolution of rocky planets, combining numerical modelling with theoretical analysis.

### What We Offer

- 4-year fully funded PhD position
- Competitive salary according to Dutch collective labour agreement (CAO)
- Excellent computing facilities and access to national supercomputing resources
- A vibrant international research environment
- Generous travel budget for conferences and collaboration visits

### What We Look For

- MSc degree (or equivalent) in physics, astrophysics, planetary science, geophysics, or a related field
- Strong background in physics and mathematics
- Programming experience (Python preferred)
- Interest in planetary formation and evolution
- Good command of English (written and spoken)
