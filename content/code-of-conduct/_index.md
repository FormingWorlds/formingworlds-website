## Our Commitment

The Forming Worlds Lab is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or technical experience.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate of others
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Offensive verbal comments related to personal characteristics
- Deliberate misgendering or use of rejected names
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sustained disruption of talks, meetings, or other events
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact Tim Lichtenberg (tim.lichtenberg@rug.nl) or the Kapteyn Institute's confidential advisor.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Forming Worlds Lab spaces, including group meetings, workshops, and online communications (email, Slack, GitHub).
